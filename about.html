About Us

Lawrence Technological University

Our Mission

LTU SPS's mission is to build a community of people interested in science and math who are helping each other and having fun doing so. This includes:

  • Community: SPS is open to everyone, and is a space for people to meet each other and form friendships.

  • Collaboration: SPS members help each other and the LTU community grow academically and succeed in their studies.

  • Fun: SPS organizes social events to allow the LTU community to get together in both academic and non-academic settings and have a break from studying.
